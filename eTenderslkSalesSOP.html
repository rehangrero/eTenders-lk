eTenders.lk Sales SOP - New Leads + Renewals

Single-page guide for sales calls: New Leads (qualification + invoice + activation) and Renewals (guide online payment + post-payment Xero invoicing).

New Lead Process Infographic Call flow + Xero + Admin

Call Flow (New Lead)
  1. Pre-call: Open lead in eTenders AdminReview company, contact, phone, email IDs, category (if available).
  2. Qualification question“Are you interested in applying for government/private tenders or receiving tender information in Sri Lanka?”
  3. If NOT interested (Admin action order)Update account manager email → set account inactive (Not Interested) → add note.
  4. If interested: Explain serviceDaily alerts (3 categories, 3 email IDs), platform login, filters by date/company/category.
  5. Subscription confirmationAsk “Would you like to subscribe?” → confirm term (6/12/24 months).
  6. Mention payment methods after verbal confirmationBank transfer, cheque, cash deposit, debit/credit card (online).
  7. End the call“We will send the invoice shortly and follow up with you tomorrow.”
Post-Call: Xero + Activation
  1. Xero: Create new contactUse exactly the same details as eTenders Admin. No duplicates/variants.
  2. Xero: Create invoiceSelect pre-uploaded subscription line item: 6 months / 1 year / 2 years.
  3. Xero: Approve & SendApprove and send on the same invoice screen to client email.
  4. Admin: Update account manager emailEnsure your official eTenders email is assigned.
  5. Payment confirmationClient emails proof or WhatsApp proof. If online card payment, Admin auto-updates.
  6. Xero: Record payment (always)Mark invoice paid, payment date = actual funds received date.
  7. Admin activation (only non-online payments)Activate subscription and set end date = 6/12/24 months from payment received date.
Online card payments auto-update eTenders Admin subscription/payment. For online payments: update Xero only and do not manually activate subscription in Admin.

New Lead Call Script Read and follow

Hi, this is Rehan from eTenders.lk. Are you interested in applying for government or private tenders, or receiving information on tenders available in Sri Lanka? If yes: We provide daily tender alerts for up to 3 categories and up to 3 email IDs, plus full access to the eTenders.lk platform where you can filter tenders by publish date, company, and category/product/service. Would you like to subscribe? Subscription plans: - 6 Months: LKR 20,000 - 1 Year: LKR 35,000 - 2 Years: LKR 65,000 Once you confirm the term, payment can be made via bank transfer, cheque, cash deposit, or debit/credit card (online). We will send you the invoice shortly and follow up with you tomorrow.
Admin notes (Not Interested path):
Update Account Manager Email ID → Set account Inactive (Not Interested) → Add note “Contacted - not interested.”

Renewal Process Infographic Client self-renew online + Xero invoice

Client Side: Online Renewal
  1. Log in to eTenders.lkExisting username and password.
  2. Go to My ProfileFrom top menu or account icon.
  3. Open My SubscriptionsSubscription section under profile.
  4. Click Renew / Make PaymentStarts checkout.
  5. Apply discount code at checkoutConcession for completing renewal online.
  6. Pay online and completeDebit card / credit card payment.
Internal Team: After Client Pays
  1. eTenders Admin: Check Payment RecordsConfirm successful transaction.
  2. Xero: Create new invoice (renewal)Contact already exists in Xero for renewing clients.
  3. Select correct subscription line item6 months / 1 year / 2 years, as renewed.
  4. Record payment and mark invoice PaidPayment date must match actual payment received date.
  5. Transaction completeOnline renewal auto-updates Admin subscription details.
Renewals are guided as an online payment. Admin subscription/payment updates automatically on successful online checkout. For renewals: create invoice and mark paid in Xero.

Renewal Call Script Guide them to pay online

Hi, this is Rehan from eTenders.lk. I’m calling regarding your subscription renewal. Are you in front of a computer or mobile now so I can guide you to renew online? It takes about 2 minutes. 1) Please log in to your eTenders.lk account. 2) Go to My Profile. 3) Open My Subscriptions. 4) Click Renew / Make Payment. 5) At checkout, enter the discount code: {CODE} and proceed with card payment. Once the payment is completed, your renewal is updated automatically in the system. Could you please confirm you received the payment success message or confirmation email? Thank you. Your renewal is completed.
Internal use: Renewal call guide + post-payment Xero steps.
